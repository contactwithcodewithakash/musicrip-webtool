Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

At MusicRip, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details our practices regarding user information, data collection, and file storage for our online media downloading utility.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MusicRip is designed to operate without collecting personal identifying information. Specifically:

  • We do not require accounts, usernames, email registrations, or profile setup.
  • We do not log or save histories connecting your IP address with the media URLs you download.
  • We do not require payment details, as our converter is entirely free.

2. Browser Storage (LocalStorage)

We provide a "Download History" feature that displays your recent downloads. This list is saved entirely inside your own browser's LocalStorage. This data is never transmitted to our servers and can be wiped completely at any time by clicking the "Clear History" button on the interface.

3. File Processing and Automatic Cleanup

When you download media using our service, the file is fetched, processed, and stored temporarily on our server. To ensure your absolute privacy and save storage capacity, we implement strict automated purge systems:

  • Every completed file is deleted immediately and permanently from our server disk space once you finish downloading it.
  • If a file is prepared but not downloaded (e.g. if the tab is closed mid-way), it is automatically purged by a cron sweeping task 30 minutes after generation.

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

We may use cookies or lightweight browser analytics tools to monitor site performance, bounce rates, and traffic flows. This metadata is aggregate, non-personal, and used exclusively to optimize conversion speeds and frontend UX layout.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our service provides converters for links pointing to third-party domains (such as YouTube and Instagram). Once you leave our site or access content from these platforms, your activity is subject to their respective Terms of Service and Privacy Policies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as legal regulations or backend infrastructure shifts. Check this page periodically for revisions.
