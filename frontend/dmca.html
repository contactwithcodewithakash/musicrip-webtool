DMCA Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

MusicRip respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. We respond to clear notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") and international copyright guidelines.

1. Operations of Our Transcoding Engine

MusicRip acts as a transient technical service provider (transcoding pipeline). Specifically:

  • We do not host, store, catalog, index, or archive any media files or streaming databases.
  • All download conversions are cached on server disks temporarily and deleted immediately upon download, or purged automatically after 30 minutes.
  • We do not control what media links are input into the converter by our users.

2. Requesting Take-down Notices

Since we do not host any content, a traditional takedown request is generally not applicable, as there is no file online for us to delete. However, if you are a copyright owner or an agent authorized to act on their behalf, and you believe that our tool is enabling copyright abuse, you can contact us at dmca@musicrip.ai.

To submit a valid DMCA request, please include the following details:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Identification of the material that you wish to restrict, including the source URL (e.g. YouTube or Instagram links).
  • Your contact information (name, address, email address, and phone number).
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized.
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

3. Counter-Notification

If you believe that your media link was incorrectly blocked or flagged as unauthorized, you may submit a counter-notice containing relevant licensing details to our email address for validation.
